Diabetes Mellitus

Diabetes mellitus is a chronic medical condition in which the body is unable to properly regulate blood glucose levels.

Symptoms:

  • Increased thirst and urination
  • Extreme fatigue
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Blurred vision

Management:

Healthy diet, exercise, blood sugar monitoring, and medications as prescribed.

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Hypertension is a condition where the blood pressure in the arteries is persistently elevated, increasing the risk of heart disease and stroke.

Symptoms:

  • Often asymptomatic
  • Headaches
  • Dizziness
  • Shortness of breath

Management:

Lifestyle modifications, regular monitoring, and medications if necessary.

Asthma

Asthma is a chronic condition that affects the airways, causing episodes of wheezing, breathlessness, chest tightness, and coughing.

Symptoms:

  • Wheezing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest tightness
  • Coughing, especially at night or early morning

Management:

Inhalers, avoiding triggers, and regular monitoring of lung function.
